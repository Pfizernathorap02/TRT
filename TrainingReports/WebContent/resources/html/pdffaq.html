A6. Within the Report Tool, you can email specific Colleagues by checking the box for each Colleague
you want to email. When you hit the "Send Email" button, an email will automatically open Outlook with a
pre-populated subject line that reads "Powers Driving Force Follow-up". You can then customize the body
of the email text before sending

Q7. What do the Overall Training Status symbols mean?

A7.

Q12.   What are Home Study and PLC Reports?
A12.   Home study refers to the study done at the attendee's home after receiving the product training material shipment
and completing the phase I pedagogue exam. PLC study refers to a classroom instructor based
product study done at Pfizer Learning Center. This includes Phase II pedagogue test and a Sales Call Evaluation.
These reports show completion/incompletion of TSRs and PHRs training at the various phases.
