Q1. What is the Training Report Tool (TRT) and what information can be found on it?
A1. This Web-based reporting tool has been designed to track your team's completion of required pre-POA Breeze viewing.

Back To Top
Q2. How is information in the TRT organized?
A2. First, the data in the Training Report Tool are arranged hierarchically—that is, by AVP, RM, DM, ARM levels. A Pfizer Leader can only access and view results specific to the team(s) they manage. Second, data are reported for each of the Business Unit’s respective products. Q3. Can I view detailed results for a specific Sales Colleague?
A3. Yes, the TRT is designed so that you can view results for a specific Colleague. In addition to Colleague names, each Colleague is listed by their District, Role (eg, TSR, PHR), Emplid (eg, Pfizer Employee ID), and Team (eg, Searle)

You can also do specific searches by using the "Search" feature located in the upper right-hand corner of the Report Tool

Q4. What are some key benefits of the TRT?
A4. The primary benefit is that depending on your level (eg, AVP, RM, ARM, DM), you will be able to determine the number of Colleagues who completed pre-POA Breeze viewings.

Q6. How does the "Send Email" feature in the Report Tool work?
A6. Within the Report Tool, you can email specific Colleagues by checking the box for each Colleague you want to email. When you hit the "Send Email" button, an email will automatically open Outlook with a pre-populated subject line that reads "Powers POA Follow-up". You can then customize the body of the email text before sending.

Q7. What do the Overall Training Status symbols mean?
A7.
Complete  Colleague has completed the preparation work.
Not Complete  Colleague has not completed the preparation work.
On Leave  Colleague is currently on leave.

Q8. Who do I contact if I have questions?
A8. Contact information for questions can be found by clicking on the "Contact" link in the upper left-hand corner of the Report Tool. You will be directed to contact Training Logistics (by phone: 1-866-4LD-2WIN; or via email: traininglogistics@pfizer.com)

Q9. I would like to review the results in more detail. Can I download Training Report Results to an Excel File?
A9. Yes, there is a link on the Training Report Tool that allows you to download results in Excel file format.

Q10. I have been following up with the necessary steps in working with my Team, so why do I keep getting reminder (follow-up) emails?
A10. The Training Report Tool is designed to send updated "reminder" emails until all of the necessary follow-up steps with Colleagues are completed by the Pfizer Leader.
