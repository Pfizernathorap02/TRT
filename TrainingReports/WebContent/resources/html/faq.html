Q1. What is the FFT Training Report Tool and what information can be found on it?
A1. This Web-based reporting tool has been designed to provide access to your team's FFT Product Training results. Results include: Pedagogue Exams, Attendance, and Sales Call Evaluation (SCE), and Overall Training Status. For colleagues not completing each requirement, coaching recommendations and tools are provided within the Web site

Q3. Can I view detailed results for a specific Sales Colleague?
A3. Yes, the FFT Training Report Tool is designed so that you can view results for a specific Colleague. In addition to Colleague names, each Colleague is listed by their District, Role (eg, TSR, PHR), Emplid (eg, Pfizer Employee ID), and Team (eg, Parke-Davis)

You can also do specific searches by using the "Search" feature located in the upper right-hand corner of the Report Tool

Q4. What are some key benefits of the FFT Training Report Tool?
A4. The primary benefit is that depending on your level (eg, SVP, VP, RM, or DM), you will be able
to determine the number of Colleagues who completed FFT Product Training, as well as the number
of Colleagues who need additional development or need to attend training. Another benefit is
that the Report Tool provides detailed information so that you-as a Pfizer Leader-will know what
specific developmental steps are required (eg, Transitional Training, Coaching, etc). The Report
Tool also indicates those Colleagues who need to complete Sales Call Evaluations
(SCEs). Finally, the site has coaching resources so that you can work with individual team members on specific developmental opportunities

Q8. What do the Sales Call Evaluation (SCE) scores mean, and what actions are required of me?
A8. Demonstrated Competence (DC)
  • Colleague demonstrated ability to meet the expectations of the Sales Call Evaluation (SCE)
Needs Improvement (NI)
  • Colleague received a score of NI on 2 or more of the selling skills or received an NI and UN on any of the selling skills
    • Actions Required: Click SCE score and review Guest Trainer comments on areas of development, and provide appropriate coaching on future field rides
Unacceptable (UN)
  • Colleague received a score of UN on 2 or more selling skills or received a "No" on Healthcare Law Compliance
    • Actions Required: Click SCE score and review Guest Trainer comments, review promotional materials, role-play, and administer new SCE
Not Taken
  • If a Colleague did not complete the required Sales Call Evaluation (SCE), the Report Tool will display "Not Taken"
    • Actions Required: Conduct Sales Call Evaluation (SCE)

Q12. What do the Overall Training Status symbols mean?

A12.
  • Complete - Colleague completed all 3 requirements: Pedagogue Exams, Attendance, and Sales Call Evaluation
  • Not Complete - Colleague did not complete one or all of the requirements: Pedagogue Exams, Attendance, or Sales Call Evaluation
  • On Leave - Training is required. Please contact your Regional Sales Office

Q13. What do the Product Exam Score symbols mean?

A13.
  • >=80% - Colleague received >= 80% on exam. No action required
  • <80% - Colleague did not score > 80% on exam. Recommended coaching: Review Learning System modules via Learning Systems Online (LSO) and complete progress check questions
  • Not Complete - Colleague did not take Pedagogue Exam. Inform colleague to take exam. If exam cannot be accessed (technical issues), please contact Dendrite Help Desk:

A18. The FFT training Report Tool only displays data for those Colleagues who actually attended FFT Product Training. So, it is quite possible that the # of Colleagues displayed on the Report Tool is actually less than the # of Colleagues in your Region (District)

Q20. When can an "Exemption" be given for a Colleague whose Overall Training status is "Not Complete"?
A20. There are two conditions where an "Exemption" is allowed:
  • If the Colleague has sold the particular product within the past 12 months
  • If the Colleague has been recently promoted or changed teams to a new / lateral TSR position
